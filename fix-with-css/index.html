Explanation

When you first the livedigi.com website you will first see an image link as their banner. This image link is actually a carasol of images with arrows which are realativly hard to see. This affects the learnability of the website, because due to the arrows being hard to see the users wont even realize that there are more images in the carasol. Although it may not seem like a big issue, some of the links in the different images of the carasol lead to different places.
In order to fix this problem we will add more simple css to highlight the arrows to let the users know that there are arrows there which means that there are more images awaiting to be discovered.

Explanation

Explanation

Ads always has a potential to compromising safety in privacy and the dimensions of usability. Fortunatly for me, the ads have been personilized due to my recent searches. But for new users the ads could be a battlegrounds for anything malicous or explicit. Especially in a website for children, where the users are more likely to click on the ads accidentally or intentionally. Even for more knowledgable users, clicking on ads will prompt no confirmation, so accidentally clicking on a malicous ad is a unforgiving death sentence.
To fix this, we will hide the ads that could pose a threat to the children or unfortunate victims who accidentally clicked them.
